Welcome

Welcome to Colibri Collection!

As the name suggests, Colibri Collection is a collection of all 342 hummingbird species with detailed descriptions, scaled images, and more.

You can browse the collection alphabetically on the index page, view a random species, or sort by any number of things such as keywords, color, or location.

For more details about the making and purpose of Colibri Collection, check out the about page.

Enjoy the information, photos, and other features!

What to Do

Colibri Collection isn't just a database of all hummingbird species, it also has many other fun things to check out:

  • Compare species using the scaled images
  • View your checklist and collect 'em all
  • Add species to your favorites to save them
  • View some slideshows of a variety of hummingbird categories
  • Kick back and watch some hummingbird videos
  • Feeling knowledgable? Test your skills in the name match game

If you run out of things to do, feel free to browse the other resources too.
